# Problem And Solution

## Problem Statement

DeFi users discover risk too late. They can see balances and positions, but they often miss the signals that matter: deteriorating ratings, fragile dependencies, exit-liquidity pressure, smart-wallet rotations, and systemic contagion.

The result is reactive behavior. Users notice risk after the market has already moved.

## Solution Summary

Xentinel turns live DeFi risk intelligence into an early-warning workflow.

Users paste a wallet, then Xentinel helps them:

- Understand current portfolio risk
- Stress test exits and downside timing
- Compare against smart-money behavior
- Detect panic and outflow pressure
- See how risk spreads across dependencies
- Ask an AI Co-Pilot for plain-English risk guidance

The product is built to be approachable for users and rigorous enough for risk reviewers.
